1 1. 引言 (Introduction)

  • 明确问题：简要说明问题的背景和现状。
  • 适当使用数据或现象引入，吸引读者注意。
  • 提出中心论点：为什么这个问题值得关注。

范例：
People are now increasingly aware of the “digital gap” or the challenges the elderly face in a digital world. As technology advances rapidly, older adults are finding it difficult to adapt, which leaves them excluded from many aspects of modern life. The digital divide has caused numerous problems for seniors, including restricted access to services and increased isolation.

2 2. 问题分析 (Body 1: Problem Description and Causes)

  • 详细描述问题：列举问题表现，比如：老年人难以适应电子支付、线上医疗或社交媒体。
  • 分析问题的原因：技术变迁快、缺乏数字教育、设计不够友好等。
  • 强调问题带来的负面影响：如孤独感增强、生活便利性降低等。

范例：
The digital gap mainly arises from several factors. First, many elderly people lack access to proper digital education or training. Second, the design of many digital tools does not take their needs into account, making it difficult for them to use smartphones or apps. Furthermore, rapid changes in technology leave little time for older generations to adapt. As a result, many elderly individuals experience emotional isolation and struggle with everyday tasks that are now primarily done online, such as shopping and medical appointments.

3 3. 解决方案 (Body 2: Solutions)

  • 提出针对性的解决方案。
  • 可以从政府、社区和家庭三个层面分别讨论。
  • 具体措施：例如举办免费数字培训课程、设计更简便的技术工具、鼓励年轻人帮助老年人。

范例：
To address this issue, several steps should be taken. Governments and communities can organize free digital literacy programs to help elderly individuals build basic skills. Moreover, technology companies should develop user-friendly interfaces designed specifically for seniors. Families also play a crucial role by offering guidance and encouraging intergenerational communication. With combined efforts, the elderly can better adapt to the digital world and benefit from its conveniences.

4 4. 结论 (Conclusion)

  • 总结问题的严峻性和解决问题的必要性。
  • 呼吁各方采取行动，共同缩小数字鸿沟。
  • 展望解决问题后的积极结果。

范例：
In conclusion, the digital gap is a pressing issue that affects the well-being of the elderly in many ways. It is essential for society to act collectively by providing education, developing accessible technologies, and fostering intergenerational assistance. Only through these efforts can we create an inclusive digital society where no one is left behind.

5 思路总结

这种写法清晰、结构合理，便于阅卷老师理解。你可以根据具体要求适当调整每部分的内容，但总体上遵循 问题描述—原因分析—解决方案—总结呼吁 的逻辑是最有效的写作方式。

People are now increasingly aware of the “digital gap” or challenges the elderly face in a digital world. Digitalization has become a prominent feature of current social changes in our country. Moreover, a worrisome point is that a fair number of the elderly still face the problem of “digital siege”, which should attract increasing attention nowadays.

Personally speaking, there are two reason accounting for this phenomenon. First and foremost, digital technology is rapidly updated and iterated in a short time so that it is difficult for most elderly people to keep their pace with the trend due to physical and intellectual limitations. In addition, the majority of digital technologies are based on the market needs of young people. Only the aged learn the cumbersome intelligent operation can they be able to apply the technologies, which virtually raises the technical threshold of the old people’s digital life.

According to statistics, our country is officially entering a moderately aged society. Therefore, we should spare no efforts to help the aged overcome the challenges and enjoy the benefits brought by the digitalization.
