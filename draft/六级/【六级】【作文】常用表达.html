1 替代表达合集

1.1 1. “Find it difficult to do” 的替代表达：

  • Struggle to do
    Many elderly people struggle to navigate complex digital systems.
  • Have trouble (in) doing
    Seniors often have trouble accessing online banking services.
  • Face challenges in doing
    They face challenges in adapting to new technologies.
  • Encounter difficulties in doing
    The elderly encounter difficulties in learning how to use smartphones.
  • Feel overwhelmed by
    Many older adults feel overwhelmed by rapid technological changes.

1.2 2. “Need xx to…” (需要xx能力) 的替代表达：

  • Require the ability to…
    The elderly require the ability to perform basic digital operations.
  • Be essential to…
    Digital literacy is essential to participating in modern society.
  • Depend on skills to…
    Navigating online platforms depends on the ability to use technology effectively.
  • Demand proficiency in…
    The use of digital payment methods demands proficiency in mobile apps.
  • Call for the capability to…
    Today’s digital era calls for the capability to handle online transactions.

1.3 3. “Can’t do” 的替代表达：

  • Be unable to…
    Some elderly people are unable to use modern communication tools.
  • Fail to…
    They fail to access essential online services due to lack of skills.
  • Lack the ability to…
    Many seniors lack the ability to use smartphones effectively.
  • Be hindered in doing…
    Their efforts are hindered in accessing digital platforms.
  • Find it impossible to…
    Without assistance, they may find it impossible to complete online applications.

1.4 4. “Prevent sb from doing” (类似access受限) 的替代表达：

  • Deny sb access to…
    Lack of digital skills denies the elderly access to online healthcare services.
  • Limit sb’s ability to…
    The complexity of technology limits seniors’ ability to participate in online activities.
  • Restrict sb from doing…
    Poor digital literacy restricts older adults from communicating via social media.
  • Block sb from…
    The absence of accessible technology blocks many seniors from enjoying digital conveniences.
  • Act as a barrier to…
    Technological advancements often act as a barrier to senior citizens.

1.5 “随着xxx的飞速发展” 的替代表达：

  • As technology advances at an unprecedented pace…
    As technology advances at an unprecedented pace, older generations are finding it difficult to keep up with digital trends.

  • With the ever-accelerating pace of technological innovation…
    With the ever-accelerating pace of technological innovation, digital tools are becoming essential in daily life.

  • Given the swift evolution of digital technologies…
    Given the swift evolution of digital technologies, many elderly people struggle to adapt.

  • In the face of fast-emerging technologies…
    In the face of fast-emerging technologies, society must ensure the elderly are not left behind.

  • With digital platforms becoming increasingly integrated into daily life…
    With digital platforms becoming increasingly integrated into daily life, more services are shifting online.

  • As advancements in technology reshape the way we live…
    As advancements in technology reshape the way we live, the elderly face growing challenges in accessing digital services.

  • Amid the rapid expansion of digital ecosystems…
    Amid the rapid expansion of digital ecosystems, older adults encounter barriers in using new technologies.

  • With technology evolving faster than ever…
    With technology evolving faster than ever, it is critical to help seniors adapt to digital tools.
